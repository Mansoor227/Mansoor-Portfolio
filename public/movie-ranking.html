Best Movies According to Mansoor

MY Top 3 Movies

  1. Your Name

    Your Name is a Japanese animated film about two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies. As they experience each other's lives, they form a deep connection and work together to uncover the truth behind their extraordinary circumstances and the fate that binds them.

  2. Silent Voice

    A Silent Voice is a Japanese animated film that follows Shoya, a former bully, who seeks redemption by befriending Shoko, a deaf girl he once tormented. As they navigate their past and personal struggles, the film explores themes of forgiveness, empathy, and the impact of bullying on individuals and relationships.

  3. Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

    Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse catapults Miles Morales into an exhilarating multiverse adventure. He teams up with a quirky, diverse crew of Spider-People to take on a formidable new villain. Swinging through breathtaking dimensions, Miles encounters wild challenges and uncovers the thrilling, limitless potential of the Spider-Verse
